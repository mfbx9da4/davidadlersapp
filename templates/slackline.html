What?

The simplest way to describe slacklining is: it is like tight rope walking, however, unlike tight-rope walking the rope is not made of a rigid metal cable but a dynamic peice of webbing. At high tension the webbing becomes elastic and can be used as a thin trampoline.

I started slacklining on the Christmas of 2011 in Bilbao, Spain. The first time I ever stepped on a slackline was with Alain Bilbo at Aste Nagusio festival.
